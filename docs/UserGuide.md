# User Manual (Employees, Department Heads, HR)

## 1. Signing in
1. Open `https://lms.alicia.local` on an **authorized office computer**.
2. Enter your email/username and password → click **Login**.
3. Check your email for the **6-digit OTP** (valid 5 minutes) and enter it.
4. Wrong password 3 times blocks the account for 24 hours — contact the System Administrator.
5. **Forgot password** on the login page emails a reset link; new passwords need 12+
   characters with upper/lowercase, a number and a symbol.
6. Use the moon/sun toggle for dark/light mode. Log out from the avatar menu when done.

## 2. Employee — applying for leave (CSC Form 6)
**Dashboard → Apply for Leave**
1. Your office, name, position and salary are pre-filled (Section 1–5 of Form 6).
2. Pick the **leave type** — the form shows that type's *Details of Leave* automatically
   (e.g. Vacation: Within the Philippines / Abroad + location; Sick: In Hospital /
   Outpatient + illness; Study: Master's / BAR / Board / Other).
3. Choose **inclusive dates** — working days are computed for you (weekends & holidays excluded)
   and checked against your balance. You can never file more days than you have credits for
   (for deductible types).
4. Upload **supporting documents** when required (the form lists exactly what's needed —
   e.g. medical certificate for sick leave over 5 days, Solo Parent ID, protection order…).
5. Tick **commutation** if requested, sign in the signature box, **Submit**.
6. Warnings (e.g., vacation leave filed under 5 days ahead) are shown in yellow — you may
   still submit; HR decides on override.

**Tracking:** *My Leave Requests* shows a status timeline
(Submitted → Department Head → HR → Mayor → Approved/Disapproved) with each signer's
comments. You can **cancel** a request any time before final approval, and download the
filled **CSC Form 6 PDF** once decided.

**Balances:** *My Balances* shows Vacation/Sick earned (1.25/month), used, and remaining,
plus your full history ledger.

**Notifications:** the bell icon and your email tell you about every status change.

## 3. Department Head — reviewing
**Pending Reviews** lists your department's requests. Open one to see the form, documents
and the employee's balance. Choose **Recommend Approval**, **Recommend Disapproval**, or
**Return for Revision**, add comments, sign, submit. Your dashboard charts show department
leave load by month/type.

## 4. HR — validating & certifying
**HR → Leave Requests**: after the Department Head's recommendation you **certify** the
applicant's Vacation/Sick credits and balance (Section 7-A of Form 6 — values are computed,
you confirm), may **override filing-deadline warnings** with a justification, verify
documents, then endorse to the Mayor for final action. HR also manages employees,
departments, balances (with remarks), holidays, and generates/export reports (PDF/Excel/CSV).

## 5. Final approver (Mayor seat)
**Final Review** queue: approve or disapprove, set **days with pay / without pay**, add
remarks, sign. Approval instantly deducts credits and notifies the employee.

## 6. Tips
- Yellow toast = warning, red = blocked action, green = success.
- Global search (top bar) finds employees, requests (by reference no.), departments.
- Your session logs out automatically after 30 minutes idle.
